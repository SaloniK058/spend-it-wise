# Value Proposition Section

## Where Your AI Budget Actually Goes

Most startups accumulate AI subscriptions over time:
- Cursor for coding
- ChatGPT for research
- Claude for writing
- Gemini for experiments
- API credits across multiple vendors

The result?
Duplicate tooling, oversized plans, unused seats, and inflated monthly spend.

SpendItWise analyzes your AI stack and shows:
- Which plans are overkill
- Which tools overlap
- Where cheaper alternatives exist
- How much you could save monthly and annually
