# Unit Economics

## What Is a Converted Lead Worth?

Credex earns money by helping companies purchase discounted AI infrastructure credits.

Assumption:
- Average company saves $300–$2,000/month
- Credex takes a margin on the transaction or resale spread

Estimated average annual revenue per converted customer:
- $1,500–$4,000 ARR equivalent

For modeling, assume:
- Average converted customer value = $2,400/year

## Funnel Conversion Assumptions

Assume:
- 1,000 visitors
- 20% complete audit = 200 audits
- 25% email capture = 50 leads
- 20% consultation booking = 10 calls
- 30% become paying customers = 3 customers

If each customer is worth:
- $2,400/year

Then:
- 3 customers = $7,200 ARR from 1,000 visitors

This supports relatively aggressive customer acquisition later.

## Why The Economics Work

This product has unusually favorable economics because:
- users self-qualify through the audit
- high-savings users are extremely valuable
- distribution can remain mostly organic
- AI infrastructure spending is growing rapidly

The biggest risk is low consultation conversion.

If users only want “free savings advice” without purchasing through Credex, monetization weakens significantly.

To counter this:
- the audit should clearly surface savings unlocked specifically through Credex
- consultation booking should feel high-value and personalized
- reports should emphasize ongoing optimization opportunities
